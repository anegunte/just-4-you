ANONYMOUS USERS

“I always worried someone would notice me, and then when no one did, I felt lonely.” ― Curtis Sittenfeld

Sometimes we need someone to notice the sadness in us to let out all the emotions we have bottled up without really spilling out into the world. What better way to let out those pent up emotions to people you don't know all the while keeping your identity a secret.

Our website offers a platform where you talk to random strangers who you may or may not have met, without you ever revealing your identity to them. This works both ways, you don't reveal your identity to them neither do they reveal theirs to you.

They will never find out if your the culprit or the victim.

How it works ?

We randomly generate one user out of the abundant existing users. It's all up to chance as they say one in a million, no one can tell who it is you are talking too. As the shuffle takes place, a random user is designated to another random user. The designation of users takes place only when there are two or more users, request a chat. All chats are deleted once the chat is closed. All privacy is maintained and no information is leaked provided we have the cooperation of all users.
