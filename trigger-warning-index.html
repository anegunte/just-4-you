Why is mental health important for students ?

There is a clear connection between mental health and academic performance. Helping students make the most of their education is everyone's goal. Students struggling with depression or other mental illnesses have a harder time feeling motivated, learning, concentrating, taking tests, etc.

Our goal is to help students make the most out of engineering without compromising their mental health. Their happiness is literally our top priority, we ensure to make students live their best life.

Introducing Us

Just 4 You is an online platform for the students of BMSCE to destress by venting to anonymous people online without the fear of being judged or having their personal problems exposed out in the open. Other than that we are offering professional help to those willing for it from the certified college counsellor.

We offer a place to freely talk, offer professional help, a bot you can talk to, and even a bot to enjoy and lighten your mood.
